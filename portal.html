Pool Rules
1
Snake Draft Roster Assignment
All 89 golfers ranked by DraftKings odds and distributed via snake draft in descending order. Every golfer appears on exactly one roster, giving each contestant an equal chance of winning.
2
Scoring
Your best-scoring golfer represents you in standings. Pool standings are determined by the lowest cumulative score-to-par among each contestant's roster.
3
One Winner Per Prize
Each prize is awarded to exactly one contestant. In the event of a tie in score, the tiebreaker is whichever contestant's golfer has accumulated more birdies in the tournament.
4
Roster Lock
Rosters are locked at the start of the tournament (April 9, 2026 at 10:00 AM ET) and cannot be changed once play begins.
